Welcome to ROMDUL Restaurant

In the heart of RomDul, a Khmer-style restaurant known for its traditional Cambodian dishes, two chefs stand proudly side by side. The elder brings calm experience, while the younger radiates fresh energy. Surrounded by warm wood décor and soft lighting, they embody the spirit of RomDul — where tradition meets passion on every plate.

Join us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner and indulge in our carefully crafted menu that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Cambodia and beyond.

Wonderful Experience

At RomDul, we believe that dining is not just about food, it's an experience. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted with warm smiles and a welcoming atmosphere. Our attentive staff is dedicated to ensuring your visit is memorable, whether you're here for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner. We take pride in offering exceptional service that makes every guest feel special.

Meet Our Chefs

At the heart of RomDul's kitchen is a chef who carries the soul of Khmer cuisine in every dish. With a deep respect for tradition and a passion for storytelling through food, she blends time-honored recipes with a modern touch. From hand-pounded kroeung to slow-cooked amok, every plate reflects her dedication to preserving Cambodia's culinary heritage while delighting each guest who walks through our doors.
